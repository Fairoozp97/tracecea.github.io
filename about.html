Who We Are

Connect Communications was established in 2005 and we are the largest Internet & Data Service Provider in Karachi. We offer various services to the corporate and consumer sectors.

We have built a stronghold for various network services on local link, such as super nodes, LAN and MAN to provide maximum performance and high scalability to take full advantage of gigabit network. Connect is determined to provide state of the art broadband communication to each house-hold as well as corporate customer at a very low cost.

Connect network is widely used in demanding markets such as service provider, financial services, media, health care, retail and government.

What makes us different?

Our fiber optic network provides the foundation for our offerings, providing metro access, IP bandwidth services, data connectivity to the major business centers in Karachi. Connect portfolio of managed services solutions enable enterprise customers to rise above legacy networks to create truly customized door-to-door optic networks. Connect enables real-time information exchange wherever and whenever it's needed. In addition to highly innovative and experienced people in the industry, Connect has a broad portfolio of assets that seamlessly interconnect to offer a full range of information exchange services.

  • Metro Area Networks (MAN)
  • Wide Area Networks (WAN)
  • Metro Ethernet
  • Layer 2 Virtual Private Network (L2VPN)
  • Internet Protocol Virtual Private Network (IPVPN)
  • Content Sharing
  • Co-location Facility
  • Network Solutions

Connect private metropolitan fiber network enables its customers to solve critical issues, including cost containment, security and reliability. Connect provides private secure communications with virtually unlimited capacity and flexibility at competitive rates. Secure optical connectivity frees companies from the constraints of tariffed. Its door-to-door optical connectivity translates into improved efficiency and productivity - resulting in substantial cost savings and higher profitability.
